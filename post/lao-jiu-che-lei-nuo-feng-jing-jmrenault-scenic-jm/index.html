【老旧车】雷诺 风景JM\Renault Scenic JM

2021-07-17 RENUALT ， 老旧车

雷诺 风景
在2004年至2009年和2011年至2013年期间，雷诺中国公司以进口的方式销售过风景JM和风景JZ。早期，风景JM引进过1.6 AT、2.0 RX4、2.0 5座和2.0 7座（大风景）四个版本。后期，2.0 RX4和1.6 AT被取消，只保留另外两款。风景JZ则至引进过2.0 5座和2.0 7座（大风景）两个版本。
拍摄于2021年7月！

btw：同时期，欧宝、大众、雪铁龙也向中国导入了赛飞利、途安、开迪、毕加索，中国的小型家用mpv市场开始进入起步阶段。

2023年1月14日更新：
在三江雷诺项目停滞后雷诺曾经与北汽集团下属的北京汽车工业公司就国产雷诺风景（当时用的是Megane·Scenic的音译，即梅戈·塞尼克）进行过磋商，根据中国知网给出的资料（雷诺计划在华生产新型轿车、北京雷诺汽车项目取得进展），“北京雷诺”项目计划投产的是使用1.6升双燃料发动机的车型并且“样车即将抵京”。

但是，在1999年12月，在法国马特拉-马可尼太空公司（Matra Marconi Space）宣布 将向台湾出售军民两用观测卫星后该计划戛然而止，相关资料也难觅于互联网。

Renault Scenic
From 2004 to 2009 and from 2011 to 2013, Renault China imported and sold Scenic JM and Scenic JZ in China. In the early years, Scenic JM introduced four versions: 1.6AT, 2.0 RX4, 2.0 5-seat and 2.0 7-seat (Grand Scenix). Some years later, 2.0 RX4 and 1.6AT were cancelled , two other models retained. Scenix JZ has introduced two versions: 2.0 5 Seat and 2.0 7 Seat (Grand Landscape).
Shooting in July 2021!

btw：In the same time，Opel、VW、Citroen introudced Zafira、Touran、Caddy、Picasso into China，it is a beginning of small size mpv "take off" in China.

January 14, 2023 Updated:
After the stagnation of the Sanjiang-Renault project, Renault had been went talks with Beijing Automotive Industry Corporation, which is a subsidiary of Beijing Automotive Industry Group, about domestic Renault Scenic ii (then known as Megane·Scenic, or "Meggo Senik"). According to the information provided by CNKI （like ElsevierScience ）（Renault plans to make new model in China、Progress has been made in the Beijing Renault Car project）, The Beijing Renault project plans to produce a 1.6-litre dual-fuel engine model and "prototypes will arrive in Beijing soon".

But the project came to an abrupt end in December 1999, when French firm Matra Marconi Space announced plans to sell civili&millitary dual-use observation satellites to Taiwan, and any information about this projectwas hard to find on the Internet.
