2023-07-29 RENUALT ， 老旧车

雷诺风朗
风朗最早出现在2004年，当时是以概念车身份出现的，2009年正式推出的Fluence量产版。 风朗和梅甘娜三厢版一样是构建于雷诺-日产C平台，这个平台上也诞生了两厢梅甘娜、奇骏、科雷傲、逍客等车型；
但风朗和梅甘娜三厢版的市场定位与两厢版梅甘娜大相径庭，三厢版梅甘娜在从土耳其生产，其主要针对的是东欧、中亚市场；而风朗则主要针对亚洲市场，其中马来西亚市场上销售的是通过ckd方式进行组装生产的，而中国市场的风朗则产自雷诺-三星汽车位于韩国釜山的生产线，售价为13.98万-18.28万元。
拍摄于2023年7月！

Renault Fluence
The Fluence first appeared as a concept car in 2004, and production version was officially launched in 2009. Both Fluence and Megan hatchback all built on the Renault-Nissan C platform, which also also underpins Megane seden and Koleos and other models;
However, the market positioning of Fluence and Megana sedan is very different from the hatchback version of Megana, sedan is produced from Turkey and mainly targets Eastern Europe and Central Asia. The Flunce is mainly targeted at the Asian market, which the Malaysian market is sold by CKD assembly production, while the Chinese market Fluence is produced by Renault-Samsung production line in Busan, South Korea, and priced from 139,800 to 182,800 Yuan.
