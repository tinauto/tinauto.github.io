2023-09-23 CHEVROLET ， 老旧车

雪佛兰 C/K1500
雪佛兰公司的“皮卡历史”始于1947年发布了第一代皮卡产品Chevy Advance Design，
1960年发布的C/K是第三代产品，“C”是两轮驱动车型，“K”是4轮驱动车型。在整个生产过程中，C/K提供了两轮驱动或四轮驱动，他们提供了轻型，中型和重型型号的C/K，所有型号都有六，V6和V8发动机。C/K共销售了四代:第一代(1960-1966)，第二代(1967-1972)，第三代(1973-1987)，第四代(1988-1998)。C/K系列停产后，雪佛兰使用Silverado（索罗德）作为下一代皮卡的名字。
拍摄于2016年4月！

===
Chevrolet C/K1500
Chevrolet's "pickup history" began in 1947 with the Chevy Advance Design, and the C/K which released in 1960 was the third ,“C” is 2-wheel drive models, “K” is 4-wheel drive models. Throughout their production life, C/Ks were offered in two or four wheel drive, they offered the C/K in light、medium、 and heavy duty models, all available with six, V6, and V8 engines. four generations were sold: 1st Gen (1960-1966)、2nd Gen (1967-1972)、and 3rd Gen (1973-1987)、4th Gen(1988-1998) . After discontinued , Chevrolet used Silverado named next generation pickup.
Shooting in April 2016！
