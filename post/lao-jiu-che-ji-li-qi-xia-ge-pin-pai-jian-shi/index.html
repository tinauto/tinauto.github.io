【历史&趣闻】吉利旗下各品牌简史（下）\A brief history of Geely"brand (Downward)

2022-07-30 历史&趣闻 ， GELLY

2009年起，中国启动新能源汽车发展战略。
同年9月，康迪车业正式发布“国内首款电动微型车” —康迪小电跑。这款车虽然叫“跑车”可是跟跑车没有一点关系，它是一辆彻头彻尾的货车。康迪车业本身没有生产乘用车的资质，但是康迪车业有汽车改装的资质和货车底盘的生产资质，金华康迪新能源车辆有限公司（康迪车业子公司）从康迪车业采购底盘，又利用康迪车业的改装资质和当时的法规漏洞（在当时厢式货车并不需要用铁板封闭货仓，只需要在乘员舱和货仓做一个隔断即可）在康迪车业生产了康迪纯电动厢式运输车 KD5010XXYEV 【车辆信息】 （其中的kd代表康迪、5代表了该车属于改装车）。

同年10月，KD5010正式以Kandi Coco的名字出口美国，经过联邦和州两级补贴之后这款车的售价只有865美元。

2012年，康迪放弃代工模式转而与众泰合作生产达成合作协议，由众泰代工生产JNJ6290EV（KD5010的乘用车版本）。

2013年3月，吉利旗下的上海华普汽车和康迪车业签署了合资协议，按各拥有50%的股份投资成立康迪电动汽车公司。同年6月，由吉利代工的JL7001BEV获得产品公告。

2013年11月，康迪电动汽车项目落户如皋（如皋的四大失败汽车项目之一，另外三个分别是陆地方舟项目、赛麟项目、庞大“水-氢”项目）。

2013年底， SMA7000BEV（JNJ6290\KD5010的150公里版本）和SMA7001BEV（JL7001BEV的150公里版本）就“入围”了 工信部 第52批节能新能源汽车汽车示范推广应用工程推荐的车型。同样在2013年，浙江左中右电动汽车服务有限公司不声不响的成立了，其主营业务为电动汽车分时租赁。

2016年初，国家开始严查系能源车骗补。经查，康迪电动车的销量中的大部分都是被上文所述的“左中右”购买的，而“左中右”正是吉利的控股子公司。吉利与康迪通过左手倒右手的生意骗取了大量的补贴。

2019年下旬，康迪电动汽车进行股权调整，康迪科技出让28%股权给吉利，吉利股权比例增加到78%。

2020年4月10日，枫叶汽车正式宣布成立，并带来旗下首款新车枫叶30X（远景x3电动版）。

2021年，康迪出让手中22%股份，吉利成为100%控股股东.

In 2009, China has launched “ new energy vehicle development strategy”.

In September 2009, Kandi Auto industry released "the first domestic electric minicar" - Kandi small electric roadster. Although the car is called a roadster, it has nothing relationship with roadster car. Because，It is a truck 。Kandi vehicle industry do not have the passenger car production qualification, but Kandi vehicle industry have modification qualification and truck chassis production qualificationf, So, JinHua Kandi new energy vehicle company (Kandi subsidiary) buy chassis from kandi , and using Kandi modified qualification and regulation (at that time, van does not need to use iron plate closed warehouse window , it only need to make a partition between the crew and the cargo ) produced KD5010 in Kandi Auto industry 【Vehicle Information】 (KD stands for kandi and 5 represents that the car belongs to the modified car).

In October 2009, the KD5010 was exported to the American as the name of Kandi Coco, selling price just $865 after federal and state subsidies.

In 2012, Kandi signed a agreement with Zotye to produce JNJ6290EV (the passenger car version of KD5010).

In March 2013, Geely's Shanghai Huapu Auto and Kandi Auto signed a joint venture agreement to set up Kandi Electric Vehicle Company with a 50% stake each. In June of the same year, JL7001BEV, manufactured by Geely, received product announcement.

In November 2013, the Kandi electric Vehicle project was launched in Rugao (one of Rugao's four failed car projects, the other three is the greenwheel Project, the Sallen Project, and the pangda "Water-Hydrogen" project).

At the end of 2013, SMA7000BEV (150km version of JNJ6290 \ KD5010) and SMA7001BEV (150km version of JL7001BEV) were "shortlisted" as models recommended by the 52nd batch of energy-saving new energy vehicle demonstration and application project of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology. Also in 2013, Zhejiang Left-Middle-Right Electric Vehicle Service company. was established , and its main business is time-sharing rental of electric vehicles.

At the beginning of 2016, the state began to investigate the fraud situaiton in energy vehicle area. It turns out that， the majority of Kandi's EV sales are being brought by the "Left-Middle-Right" company, which is Geely's majority-owned subsidiary. Gilley and Kandy defrauded large number of subsidies by selling car from right hand to the left hand.

in late 2019, Kandi Technology sold 28% equity to Geely, Geely's equity ratio increased to 78%.

On April 10, 2020, Maple Leaf Motors announced its establishment, and brought its first new car Maple 30X (Yuanjing X3 electric version).

In 2021, Kandi sold 22% of its shares and Geely became the 100% controlling shareholder.
