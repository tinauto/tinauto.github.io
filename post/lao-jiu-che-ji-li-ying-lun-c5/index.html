【老旧车】英伦 c5\Englon c5

2022-07-16 GELLY ， 老旧车

请先阅读吉利品牌简史（上），了解基本背景

英伦c5
英伦c5就是一款经历了这三个时代的车型，c5的前身是2009年发布的“全球鹰gc5”。但是，在2010年11月正式发布的时候吉利却将gc5给了英伦，其两厢版命名为英伦sc5-rv、三厢版为英伦sc5、跨界版为英伦sx5（三厢版和跨界版没有正式销售）。2014年吉利进行品牌战略调整后将sc5改名为c5，并且发布了两厢版和三厢版车型。2017年，吉利再次进行品牌战略调整后吉利将英伦c5停产，同时将“英伦sx5”跨界车复活并更名为远景x3来销售（2020年吉利发布x3的电动版“枫叶30x”，2021年吉利发布x3升级版x3pro并同时发布“枫叶30x”的换壳版“几何ex1”）。
拍摄于2022年1月！

btw：借壳还“名”-2013年，吉利旗下的上海华普汽车和康迪汽车签署了合资协议，按各拥有50%的股份投资成立康迪（电动汽车）公司。2016年，康迪（电动汽车）进行股权调整。而其股权调整后生产第二款车型就是全球鹰logo的电动版英伦c5（康迪k17\k17a）。至此，这款车在经历了7年之后终于挂上了原本应该属于自己的logo。

Englon C5
First，please read A brief history of Geely"brand (Upward)，understand the background。
The Englon C5 is a model that has experienced these three eras. The predecessor of the C5 is the "Global Hawk GC5" that released in 2009. However, when it was officially released in November 2010, Geely named englon to GC5 . The hatchback version was named Englon SC5-RV, the seden version was Englon SC5, and the crossover version was Englon SX5 (the hatchback version and the crossover version were not officially sold). In 2014, Geely adjustment of brand strategy and changed the name of SC5 to C5 , and released hatchback and seden models. In 2017, Geely stopped production of Englon C5 after adjusting its brand strategy again, and revived "Englon SX5" crossover and renamed it yuanjing X3 for sale (In 2020, Geely released "Maple leaf 30X", an electric version of X3, In 2021, Geely released an updated X3 -“X3Pro” and released a replacement shell version of "Maple Leaf 30X" -"Geometry EX1" at the same time).
Shooting in January 2022!

btw：get the name back- In 2013, Geely's subsidiary -Shanghai Huapu and Kandi Auto signed a joint venture agreement to creat kandi (electric vehicle) with 50% of the shares by each. In 2016, Kandi (electric vehicle) shares adjustment. After the adjust ,the second model they made is the Global Eagle logo electric version of Englon C5 (K17 \ K17A). It took seven years to finally get its own logo.
