蓝旗亚 Thesis
在介绍阿尔法罗密欧156的文章中我曾经提过，“南京菲亚特曾通过自己的渠道展示过蓝旗亚Thesis但是没人购买”。但是，事实证明我错了！

南京菲亚特在2003年的上海车展上除了展示派力奥、西耶那之外，还展示了西耶那Stilo Abarth、西耶那Stilo Multiwagon、Doblo多宝、派力奥拉力赛车和蓝旗亚Thesis等5款，其中蓝旗亚是首次在中国登场。在此之后的多次车展中南京菲亚特也曾多次展出过这款车，在2004年还一度传出过南京菲亚特要以80万的售价国产这款车。但是，最终这款车和难产多宝一样的消失在了历史长河中！

我曾经一度认为在国内没有私人牌照的蓝旗亚Thesis（意大利大使馆有一辆公务车），但是在查询其他资料我偶然发现国内除了意大利大使馆的那辆公务车意外还有一辆私人牌照的Thesis，并且还有人做过评测 https://www.bilibili.com/video/av64650582/。

Lancia Thesis
In the article which introducing Alfa Romeo 156, I once mentioned that "Nanjing Fiat once showed Lancia Thesis through its own channels, but no one bought it". But I was proved wrong!

At the 2003 Shanghai Internation Auto Show, in addition to the Perio and Siena, Nanjing Fiat also exhibited five models , including the Siena Stilo Abarth、Siena Stilo Multiwagon、Doblo、Perio racing car, and Lancia Thesis, it is the first time which Lancia was introduced in China. After that, Nanjing Fiat also exhibited this car for many times in the other auto show. In 2004, it was once rumored that Nanjing Fiat would make this car in China at the price of 800,000 Yuan. However, in the end, this car and the difficult production of Duobao disappeared in the river of history!

Once,I think have no private plate Lancia Thesis in China（Italian embassy had one which with diplomacy black plate）, But, when i search other information I stumbled upon a private license plates, in addition to the Italian embassy Lancia Thesis and someone have done a review https://www.bilibili.com/video/av64650582/.
