【老旧车】三菱 3000GT\Mitsubishi 3000GT

2023-03-25 MITSUBISHI ， 老旧车

三菱 3000GT
三菱在1989年的东京车展上发布了概念车-HSX，其量产版于1990年10月正式发布，这款车就是三菱3000GT。

3000GT有三个版本可供选择，分为使用自然吸气发动机的基本款和SL款、使用双涡轮增压发动机的VR-4款。三菱3000GT最令人津津乐道的就是它所配备的众多高科技配置，6碟CD、定速巡航、电动座椅、自动空调、双安全气囊是标准配置，后期车型中更是加入无钥匙进入。在VR4\VR4S版本上更是添加了全时四驱系统、四轮转向转向、可控排气声浪调节系统、电子控制悬架，至于此外还有主动式扰流板-当车速超过72公里/时（45英里/时），扰流板会将后尾翼下调15度；

1999年,3000GT退出三菱最为倚重的美国市场。2001年,全面停产。在此之后，三菱就不再有高档跑车推出。
btw：1985年三菱与克莱斯勒合资成立的一家名为“钻石之星”（Diamond Star Motors）的汽车公司，两家公司大量共享着多项技术以及平台。在此背景之下，3000GT也有了一个北美版的孪生兄弟-道奇隐身（Dodge Stealth）

···
Mitsubishi 3000GT
Mitsubishi unveiled the concept car-the HSX, at the 1989 Tokyo Motor Show, and the production version- Mitsubishi 3000GT, was officially released in October 1990.

The 3000GT is available in three versions to choice, the base model and the SL model with a naturally aspirated engine、the VR-4 model with a twin-turbo engine. The Mitsubishi 3000GT is famous for its many high-tech features, 6-disc CD、fixed cruise、electric control seats、automatic air conditioning、doublesafety airbag is the standard configuration, later models had the keyless entry system. In addition to VR4\VR4s model, Mitsubishi added All-Wheel -Drive system、four-wheel steering (4WS) system、tunable exhaust system 、 electronic control suspension sywtem, even the “Active Aero system”-when speed excess of 45mph (72km/h), the spoiler will change the angle of the rear wing by 15 degrees.

In 1999, 3000GT was withdrawn from the US market. In 2001, all 3000GT model was discounted . Since then, Mitsubishi has had no premium sports cars.
btw：in 1985，a joint venture company named “Diamond Star Motors” was formed，as between Mitsubishi and Chrysler. The two companies share a number of technologies and platforms. In the background, the 3000GT also gets a North American version-Dodge Stealth.
