本田 Predule 第四代
1991年，本田发布了第四代了Prelude，作为肩负着击败尼桑S13使命的重要车型，该车搭载了最新的电控E-四轮转向系统E-4WS和F22b、H22a两款高压缩比发动机。但是，因为车身宽度和重量的增加导致的操控性下降，再加上早期E-4WS系统故障频发，最终导致该车延续了上一代命运，继续被s13和180sx碾压。
拍摄于2020年5月！

Honda Prelude gen 4
In 1991, Honda launched Prelude gen 4, as an important vehicle on the mission to defeat Nissan S13. It was equipped the latest E-4WS system and F22b、H22a engines . However, the manipulate feeling decreased due to the increase in width and weight, coupled the frequent failures of the early E-4WS system, eventually let the gen 4 got the same fate as gen 3, which continued to be "crushed" by the S13 and 180SX.
Shooting in May 2020！
