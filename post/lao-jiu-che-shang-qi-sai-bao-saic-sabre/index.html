【老旧车 】上汽 赛宝\SAIC Sabre

2023-06-03 SAIC ， 老旧车

上汽 赛宝
千禧年初，伴随着中国加入wto，中国的家用车市场也逐渐热络了起来，各家厂商都力求在这个潜力巨大的市场上分一杯羹。在当时“老三样”（桑塔纳、捷达、富康）独霸天下的情况下，上汽通用在2002年推出了基于欧宝可赛二代平台（Opel Corsa B）制造的赛欧，该车型在山东烟台工厂生产（该工厂原属于大宇汽车）。

同平台引进国内的除了轿车版本还有厢式货车版，该车型在江苏仪征工厂生产，定名为上汽赛宝。该车型有两个版本，SAC6420型为5座版，而SAC5020型的则是2座位版。不过高达7万元的售价和只有520公斤的准载质量注定了它水土不服的命运，其最终在2005年停产。而仪征工厂则在2006年开始生产荣威Roewe系列轿车。
拍摄于2020年5月！

SAIC Sabre
At the beginning of the millennium，with China join to the WTO , the car market in China is heating up, as manufacturers strive to get a piece of cake in this potentially huge market. At a time when the "old three" ( Santana, Jetta and Fukang-Citroen ZX) dominated the market, Saic-GM launched the Sail in 2002, which base on Opel Corsa B platform, Sail manualfactory in Shandong Yantai factory(which was owned by Daewoo auto).

The platform to introduce for the domestic in addition to the car version and van version, the van version producted in SAIC Jiangsu Yizheng factory , and named SAIC Saibao. It is two models supplied to the market, the SAC6420-five seats and the SAC5020-two seats. But with a high price 70,000 Yuan and cargo weight of only 520 kilograms, it was not fit for China market purpose, and it was halted in 2005. The Yizheng factory began production of the Roewe series in 2006.
Shooting in May 2020！
