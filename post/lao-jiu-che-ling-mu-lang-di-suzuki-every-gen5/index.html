【老旧车】铃木 浪迪\Suzuki Every

2022-10-08 SUZUKI ， 老旧车

铃木 浪迪
在介绍这款车之前有一点必须要说一下“浪迪”这个名字，因为在中国和外国浪迪（Landy）指的是不同的车型。在国外，铃木-浪迪（Landy）指的是铃木在2007年-2010年期间以尼桑Serena为基础换标生产的一款中型MPV；而在中国铃木-浪迪（Landy），则指的是铃木Every第五代。这两款车使用相同名字，生命周期也基本相同（07年-10年\11年）。

国内浪迪的原型车是第五代铃木Every，该车于2005年在日本发布。在发布仅两年之后，铃木就把这代Every带到了中国。而且，因为不用受日本本土K-Car法规的限制，所以浪迪的车身长度比原版Every多了40厘米，达到了3米85。但是，成也Every、败也Every。铃木依靠Every一代和二代带火了国内的微面市场，但也给这类车型定下了一个“廉价+工具车”的调子并获得了一个非常形象的名字-“面包车”。

2007年的中国没人认可所谓的MPV，稍微讲些“体面”的都要买一辆三厢轿车，而需要工具车的会去选五菱的微面（07年一年五菱之光年销量就达到30万）。而当铃木把一款“正宗铃木面包车”卖到了5.25-7.68万的时候就直接“铺就”了它的死亡之路。
btw：2014年后，符合面包车特征汽车，行驶证上的车辆类型统一为“小型面包车”，就此“面包车”这个名字正式转正。
拍摄于2022年6月！

Suzuki Every
Before introducing this car, it is necessary to mention the name "Landy", because Landy refers different model in China and Foreign . In foreign , Landy refers a mid-sized MPV made based on the Nissan Serena with SUZUKI logo between 2007 and 2010 ; In China, Landy refers Suzuki Every fifth generation. The two cars use the same name and have the same life (07 year - 10 \11 year).

The China-Landy prototype is the fifth-generation Suzuki Every, which was released in Japan in 2005. Two years after its release, Suzuki brought this generation Every into China. Moreover, because it is not need to abidan theK-Car regulations in Japan, the length is 40 cm longer than the original Every, reaching 3.85 m. However, the success or failure of the affair is all due to the same car. Suzuki bring the first generation and the second generation Every into China fire up the micro-minivan market, but also set a tone of "cheap + tool car" for this type car and gained a very vivid name - "Breadcar".

In 2007, no one in China approved the so-called MPV. Those who want to get a “dignity” would buy a sedan, and those who need a tool car would choose Wuling's micro-minivan (In 2007, Wuling sold 300,000 HongGuang in one year). And when Suzuki sells an "Authentic Suzuki Breadcar " for 52500-76800 Yuan, it directly "paved a death path" for himself.
btw: After 2014, the vehicle seized compliant with standards of micro-minivan will be unified as "Small-Breadcar" on the license, and the name "Breadcar" got official approbation.
