【老旧车】上海汇众 伊斯坦纳\SHAC ISTANA

2022-06-04 上海汇众 ， 老旧车

奔驰MB100这个名字最早出现在奔驰西班牙工厂生产的br.631客车身上，这款车从1981年生产到1995年，之后在1996年被奔驰VITO所取代。
1991年，双龙与奔驰公司结成战略伙伴关系。
1994年，双龙ISTANA诞生（这个名字来自于马来语，意为“飞驰的宫殿）
1997年，亚洲金融危机爆发，双龙资金链发生断裂。同年，大宇汽车收购双龙汽车。
1999年，大宇集团因为盲目扩张爆发债务违约，董事长金宇中外逃。同年，大宇集团宣布解散，双龙汽车成为独立的公司

2002年2月10日，中国石油甘肃白银公司的奔驰MB100轻型客车在行驶途中，因刹车跑偏，导致翻车，造成一死二伤的重大交通事故
2002年4月4日，甘肃省出入境检验检疫局向国家质检总局上报了关于奔驰MB100旅行车存在严重质量隐患的紧急报告
2002年5月28日，中国质量认证中心日前发布公告，暂时吊销韩国双龙公司的MB100系列旅行车的进口商品安全质量许可证书。　
2002年10月24日，恢复受理奔驰MB100轻型客车的进口报检。
　
2003年1月，上海汇众购买了MB100的生产线开始生产该车型
2004年1月，使用汇众logo的MB100正式开始销售，售价为15-20万人民币.

拍摄于2021年1月！

btw：除了常见的奔驰版、双龙版、上海汇众版以外，这车还有大宇版。双龙被大宇收购后，曾经生产过悬挂大宇logo的版本

SHAC ISTANA
Benz MB100 first appeared on the BR.631 which produced in Benz's Spanish factory from 1981 to 1995, and it was replaced by the Benz VITO in 1996.
In 1991, Ssangyong and Benz announced a strategic partnership
In 1994, Ssangyong ISTANA was born (the name comes from the Malay word for "galloping palace")
In 1997, the Asian financial crisis outbreak, Ssangyong financial chain broke. In the same year, Daewoo acquired Ssangyong
In 1999, Daewoo group chairman Kim Woo-choong fled after the company defaulted on its debt because of its blindly expansion. In the same year, The Daewoo Group announced its dissolution and Ssangyong became an independent company

On February 10, 2002, a MB100 belonging to CNPC Gansu Baiyin Company overturned due to something wrong of the brake, one man died and two others were injured in this accident
On April 4, 2002, Gansu Entry-Exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau reported an urgent report to AQSIQ about the potential quality problems in MB100
On May 28, 2002, China Product & Quality Certification Supervision Center issued a notice : temporarily revoking the import commodity safety quality license of MB100
On October 24, 2002, import resumed

In January 2003, Shanghai Huizhong Auto Company purchased the production line of MB100 and began to produce itin china
In January 2004, MB100 with the Huizhong logo went on sale at a price of 150,000-200,000 RMB.

Shooting in January 2021!

btw:Deawoo-Ssangyong produced a version with Daewoo logo after Ssangyong acquired by Daewoo
