菲亚特多宝
2003年，南京菲亚特曾欲将菲亚特多宝一代引入中国制造销售，并且进行了资格申报。2003年广州车展时也曾经展示过该车，并称将于一年内上市。可是，直到南京菲亚特破产(2007年)该车也未正式销售过。在网上能找到的资料很少，一张是喷涂了警车标志的图片、另外就是该车进行申报时的官方照片。该车有三种型号，分别是NJ7199（1.9l 7seat) 、NJ6420(2.0l 5seat)、NJ6420A(1.6l 7seat) 相关资料：https://auto-che.com/b/fiat/wagon-car.html
BTW：在2004年，一汽大众也曾经将大众开迪引进国产并且聘请成龙作为形象代言，但是直到该车2009年停产总销量也不过大约9000多辆

Nanjing-Fiat doblo
In 2003,Nanjing Fiat once wanted to introduce Fiat Dobo gen1 into China for manufacturing and sales, and has applied for qualification. It was shown at the 2003 Guangzhou Automotive Exhibition ，and said it would sale within a year. However, the car was not officially sold until Nanjing Fiat bankrupt (year of 2007). There is only little information available found from the Internet , a picture of the police car painted and some official photo of the qualification declaration. There are three models of the car，NJ7199(1.9L 7SEAT), NJ6420(1.9L 5SEAT), NJ6420A(1.6L 7SEAT) Information：https://auto-che.com/b/fiat/wagon-car.html.
BTW: In 2004, FAW-Volkswagen introduced Volkswagen Caddy to China and hired Jackie Chan as the caddy spokesman, but until the car discontinued， in the 5 years ending in 2009, the total sales volume was only about 9,000 units
