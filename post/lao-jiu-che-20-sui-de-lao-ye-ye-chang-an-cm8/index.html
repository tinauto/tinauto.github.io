2024-03-30 CHANGAN ， 老旧车

长安CM8
2004年6月的在北京国际汽车展上，长安汽车推出了“长安CM8”、概念跑车“龙腾”、概念轿车“长江鲟”三款汽车，其中的CM8作为长安第一款自主开发、具有自主知识产权的自主品牌汽车一出场就赢得了很高的关注，这款车正式结束了长安只有合资（长安铃木、长安福特）和仿制车型（长安之星面包车、st110仿制自铃木carry）的历史。
拍摄于2024年3月！

+++
Changan CM8
In June 2004, at the Beijing International Automotive Exhibition, Changan launched three car -"Changan CM8" the concept sports car "Longteng", the concept car "Yangtze Sturgeon" , of which CM8 as Changan's first self-developed, with independent intellectual property rights of the independent brand car has got high attention. This car officially ended Changan's history which only have joint ventures models (Changan Suzuki, Changan Ford) and imitation models (Changan Star minivan, st110 copied from Suzuki carry).
Shooting in March 2024!
