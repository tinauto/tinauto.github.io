【老旧车】标致607\Peugeot 607

2024-05-25 PEUGEOT ， 老旧车

标致607
2002年10月，东风集团与法国PSA集团签定了扩大合作的协议，东风-psa公司正式宣告成立。按照标致-雪铁龙与东风达成的协议,从2004年起,神龙公司开始生产标致307轿车（早期只有307三厢版，这是针对中国和土耳其市场的特供车），这意味着标致汽车在广州标致倒闭5年之后重返中国市场。而在307国产之前的这一段空档内标致也没有歇着，标致将的206cc、307cc、406coupe、607以进口方式引入到了国内。

标致607是一款源于标致605/雪铁龙XM平台开发而来的车型，在当时与奔驰E级宝马5系互为竞品。中国市场曾经销售销售过2004版和2006版两个版本，2004年款有2.2升L4与3.0升V6两个版本，售价在43万和47万；2006年推出的则只有3.0升V6一款，售价为47万元。
拍摄于2021年4月！

Peugeot 607
In October 2002, China Dongfeng Group and France PSA Group signed an agreement to expand cooperation area, Dongfeng-Psa company was officially established. According to the agreement , from 2004, Dongfeng-Shenlong began to produce the Peugeot 307 sedan (early only the 307 seden, which was a special for the Chinese and Turkish ), which meant that Peugeot returned to the Chinese market after the Guangzhou Peugeot collapse five years ago. Peugeot did not rest in the gap year before 307 “produing”, and Peugeot introduced 206cc, 307cc, 406coupe and 607 into China by import tunel.

This Peugeot 607 was developed on the Peugeot 605/ Citroen XM platform and was a competitor to the Benz E-Class and BMW 5 Series .Two versions， 2004 and 2006， have been sold in the Chinese market. In 2004 vision, there are two engine 2.2 L4 and 3.0 V6, priced at 430,000 Yuan and 470,000 Yuan. In 2006, only a 3.0 V6 engine, priced at 470,000 Yuan.
