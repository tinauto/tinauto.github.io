2021-11-06 DEAWOO ， 老旧车

大宇 贵族
乘着中国改革开放的春风，德日美车企纷纷进入中国，作为中国的近邻韩国车企自然也是不甘落后，但是因为此时韩国还未和中国建交所以一直没有机会进入到中国市场。但从1983年北京申办亚运会后，两国民间交往日趋加强。1990年，此时时中国并不富裕，当局为了解决办亚运会的资金不足的问题向社会各界募集捐助。韩国现代汽车、大宇汽车以及起亚汽车（此时现代和起亚还没有合并）立刻以非官方身份向北京政府捐赠了一批汽车， 其中有现代Excel、第二代现代索纳塔以及大宇赛手、大宇贵族等车型。该款车型有1.5l和2.0l两个排量，所有到中国销售的都是2.0l。
拍摄于2013年10月！

Daewoo Espero
With the reformming and opening , German、Japanese and American auto companies entered China one after another. As the China's close neighbor, Korean auto companies did not want to lag behind. But, Korea had not established diplomatic relations with China at that time, so they had no opportunity to enter the Chinese market. However, since Beijing bid for the Asian Games in 1983, the people-to-people exchanges between the two countries have been increasingly. In 1990, China was not rich, the authority sought donations from all channels to solve the problem of insufficient funds for hosting the Asian Games. Hyundai、Daewoo and Kia Motors (hyundai and Kia had not yet merged) immediately donated cars to the Beijing government by unofficial identity , including hyundai Excel\hyundai Sonata GEN 2, Daewoo Racing and Daewoo Espero.
This model has two engine, 1.5L and 2.0L, all sold in China are 2.0L.
Shooting in October 2013！
