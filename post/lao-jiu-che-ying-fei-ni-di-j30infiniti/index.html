【老旧车】英菲尼迪 J30\Infiniti J30

2023-10-21 INFINITI ， 老旧车

英菲尼迪j30
J30是英菲尼迪J系列的第一款车型，它的开发工作起始于1988年夏季并于1992年正式上市以接替停产的M30。因为该车是为了美国市场特点而量身定做，所以其设计全部是由日产位于美国加里福尼的团队完成。自1992年推出以来，它经历了三代车型的演变，分别是J30、J30t和Q45。

在日本，尼桑把英菲尼迪J30改名为尼桑Leopard J.Ferie以填补二代Leopard停产之后留下的空档（J.Ferie这个来源于法语Jours fériés，意为“假日”）。值得一提的是，尼桑Leopard J.Ferie除了提供与J30相同的VG30DE-3.0LV6发动机以外，还提供了J30所没有的VH41DE-4.1L V8（该款发动机同时使用在美国市场上销售的CIMA\西玛上面）。
拍摄于2013年9月！

Infiniti J30
The J30 was the first model in Infiniti's J series, j30 development work beginning in the summer of 1988 and it was officially launched in 1992 to replace the discontinued M30. Because the car was designed for the American market, all it was designed at Nissan's California team. Since its launched in 1992, it has evolved three generations-J30, J30t and Q45.

In Japan, Nissan renamed the Infiniti J30 to Nissan Leopard J.Erie to fill the gap which because of the second generation Leopard disconnected ( J.Ferie derived from the French-Jours fériés, meaning “holiday”). Nissan Leopard J.Ferie not only offering the same VG30DE-3.0LV6 engine as the J30, but also equipped the VH41DE-4.1L V8 which J30 does not (this engine is also used in the CIMA thoes sold in the US market).
