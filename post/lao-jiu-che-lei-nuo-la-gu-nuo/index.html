【老旧车】雷诺 拉古娜\Renault Laguna

2023-05-20 RENUALT ， 老旧车

雷诺 拉古娜
雷诺1993年正式进入中国市场，1994年开始三江雷诺塔菲克项目，但是该项目以失败告终。1999年，雷诺以进口的方式重启中国业务，自此之后雷诺在中国销售过风景第二代、风景第三代、梅甘娜三厢、梅甘娜硬顶敞篷车以及第二代拉古娜、第三代拉古娜（威赛帝Vel Satis虽然也在国内销售，但数量极少）。

为了替换已经销售了18年的雷诺21，雷诺于1993年发布了第一代拉古娜。在第一代车型销售了七年之后，雷诺在2000年推出了二代车型，该车和尼桑Primera使用相同平台（1999年雷诺和日产联盟成立）。2007年6月，雷诺正式发布第三代拉古娜。一年后的2008年4月20日，雷诺在北京车展上正式公布了第三代拉古那将引入中国的消息。初期，进口到中国市场的车型仅为2.0T一款，售价为27.28万。
拍摄于2023年5月！

Renault Laguna
Renault officially entered the Chinese market in 1993 and started the “ Sanjiang Renault Trafic project ” in 1994, but the project failure. In 1999, Renault restarted its China operations as an import company. Since then, Renault has imported and sold Scenic II, Scenic III, Megane sedan, Megane Hardtop convertible, Laguna II and Laguna III. (The Vel Satis are also sold in China, but the sales volume is very small.)

For replacing the Renault 21 which has been sold 18 years，Renault released the first Laguna in 1993. After seven years on the market, the Laguna was replaced with the second generation in December 2000. The second generation of the Renault Laguna used the same platform with the Nissan Primera. (the Renault-Nissan alliance was formed in 1999). In June 2007, Renault officially released the third generation Laguna. One year later, at the 2008 Beijing international Auto Show，Renault officially announced that the third generation Laguna would be introduced to China . At the beginning, only one 2.0T model was imported and with a price of 272,800 Yuan.
