2021-11-20 HAFEI ， 老旧车

哈飞赛豹
哈飞汽车成立于1994年，是哈尔滨飞机工业集团有限责任公司的子公司（2009年被长安收购），该公司产品线包括面包车、轻卡、两厢轿车、三厢轿车等。

赛豹是哈飞公司在2005年发布的该品牌的第一款三厢轿车，该车由意大利宾尼法瑞纳设计。使用三菱发动机，有1.6l和2.0l两种排量，其中1.6l的售价为8.88万和9.68万。值得一提的是，该车的后悬挂使用的是同价位不多见多连杆独立悬架。

赛豹除了有燃油版本外还有纯电动车版本-赛豹ev，赛豹ev曾经以coda ev的名义出口到美国。
日本网友的评测 http://www.sugao.jp/blog/2011/09/coda-ev.html
拍摄于2021年11月！

Hafei Saibao
Hafei Automobile founded in 1994, it is a subsidiary of Harbin Aircraft Industry Group. (acquired by Changan in 2009). The company's product line includes minivan、light truck、hatchback 、 sedan。

Saibao is the first sedan of hafei brand and it released in 2005，this car designed by Italian Pininfarina. This car use Mitsubishi 1.6L and 2.0L engine , priced of 1.6L from 88,800 yuan to 96,800 yuan. It is worth mentioning that the rear suspension of the car is multi-link independent suspension, it is rarely seen at the same price of.

In addition to the fuel version, there is also an electric version ,and was exported to the United States under the name Coda EV.
