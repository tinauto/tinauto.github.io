【老旧车】东风 风神A9\Dongfeng Aeolus A9

2022-02-26 DONGFENG ， 老旧车

东风
东风A9是东风制造的第一款中大型轿车，该车采用了与第一代雪铁龙C6相同的PF3平台。虽然使用了相同的平台，但是他们的价格和目标客户是不同的。使用1.8t发动机的A9价格低于C6（a9售价为18-22万、c6为22-28万），但它有更长的质保期(A9 5年，C6 3年)；更多的设备，如Nappa皮革的通风按摩座椅、Infinity音响、自动升降的hud等。这些配置在同价位c6上面根本买不到。而c6则有1.6t发动机的版本，售价为19-20万；但是，很遗憾这款车的销量却非常的惨淡。原因很简单，第一，年轻人没有十八万；第二，有钱的年轻人不会选择一款如此之老气横秋的车；第三，有钱的中老年人更加倾向于选择合资品牌；最终，这款车在2019年正式停产。虽然悬挂东风logo的东风a9停产了，但是使用相同平台雪铁龙C6还是一直在生产。当然，这款车的销量也是同样惨淡，2021年平均每月大约500-700辆。
拍摄于2022年9月！

Dongfeng Fengshen A9
The Dongfeng Fengshen A9 is the first mid-large size sedans what dongfeng made，and this car used same pf3 platform with the first generation Citroen C6.Although used the same platform ,but their prices and goals customer are different. A9 price is lower than C6 but it have longer warrant （A9 5 years vs C6 3 years ）；more equipment，like Airy-Nappa -Massage seat、Infinity audio、Automatic lifting hud, and so on.Those are not available on the c6 at the same price. Unfortunately, sales of this car have been very poor. The reason is simple: First, most of young people do not have 180,000 yuan for it; Second, rich young people will not choose a old school car . Third, rich middle-aged and elderly people tend to choose venture brand; Finally, the car was discontinued in 2019. Although the Dongfeng A9 with the Dongfeng logo discontinued, the twins brother-Citroen C6 was still in production. Of course, sales have been similarly dismal like a9, averaging around 500-700 units per month in 2021.
