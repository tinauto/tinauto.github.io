【老旧车】英菲尼迪G37s\Infiniti G37s

2024-06-08 INFINITI ， 老旧车

英菲尼迪g37s
2007年7月，英菲尼迪在诞生18年之后正式进入中国，其首先带来的是g35（vq35）和fx35\45，加上2004年来的雷克萨斯、2006年来的讴歌，日系三大豪华品牌正式开启了对于中国市场的争夺。
2007年纽约车展上，英菲尼迪发布了g35的升级产品，采用VQ37 3.7L V6发动机动力系统的G37系列取代了之前的G35系列。2008年12月18日，英菲尼迪G37三厢和双门版正式在国内上市，Infiniti G37 双门跑车售价为人民币66万元人民币。另外，G37四门版轿车车型也公布了售价为48.3万元。
拍摄于2024年6月！

btw：英菲尼迪G37是自上个世纪90年代M30敞篷以来英菲尼迪生产的第二款敞篷车也是首辆挂上英菲尼迪车标的敞篷跑车（m30只有尾标而没有前标）

===
Infiniti G37s
In July 2007, Infiniti officially entered China after 18 years of birth, which first brought G35 (vq35) and FX35\45 into China, In addition with Lexus which 2004 and Acura which 2006, the three Japan luxury brands opened the competition in Chinese market.
At the New York Auto Show 2007, Infiniti launched upgrade of the G35, G37 series which with the VQ37 3.7L V6 engine replacing the previous VQ35 series . On December 18 2008, the Infiniti G37 coupe and sedan were officially launched in China, and the Infiniti G37 coupe was priced at 660,000 Yuan. the sedan was at a price of 483,000 Yuan.
Shooting in June 2024!

btw:G37 is the second cabriolet by Infiniti since the M30 cabriolet in the 1990s, and the first cabriolet to use the Infiniti front logo (the m30 has a rear logo only).
