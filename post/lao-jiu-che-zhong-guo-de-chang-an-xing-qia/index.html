长安星卡
2002年7月8日，河北长安汽车有限公司正式成立。同年，开始生产名称为“长安星卡”的微型货车。

2007年河北长安发布型号为SC1022S8的小型货车，该车的长/宽/高分别是3860mm/1485mm/1870mm、
座位数为2+3人、排量为0.8l（最大功率为29kw、最大马力为40匹）、额定载货质量380kg，在货车普遍求大的国内这绝对算得上一个异类！
拍摄于2022年4月！

btw：日本规定：车辆长/宽/高分别不超过3400mm/1480mm/2000mm、座位不超过4个 、额定载货质量不超过350kg、排量不超过660ml（最大功率不超过47kw、63hp）的车辆为K-Car。

===
Changan star
On July 8, 2002, Hebei Changan Automobile Co., Ltd. was formally established. In the same year, the production of mini truck which named "Changan Star" began.

In 2007, Hebei Changan released a mini truck which model SC1022S8, the length/width/height of the car are 3860mm/1485mm/1870mm、the person capacity is 2+3、the displacement is 0.8l (maximum power is 29kw、maximum horsepower is 40hp), and the rated cargo mass is 380kg, which is an anomaly in the country where seek for “more larger”!
Shooting in April 2022!

btw：Japan govermaent stipulates that the length/width/height of the vehicle does not exceed 3400mm/1480mm/2000mm, the seat does not exceed 4、thethe rated cargo mass does not exceed 350kg，and an engine capacity less than 660cc (maximum power does not exceed 47kw) is K-Car.
