2022-09-10 MITSUBISHI ， 老旧车

三菱 戈蓝
自1969年起三菱一共开发了9代戈蓝，前八代的定位都是高性能运动四门轿车。这八代中大陆进口过5代和8代、台湾中华汽车生产过8代、港澳进口过多代。2003年的纽约车展，三菱北美分公司发布第九代戈蓝。2006年11月，东南汽车引进戈蓝于国内生产。但，当年引进的版本并不是北美市场上的原版，而是台湾三菱的Grunder版本，这版是日本三菱汽车与台湾中华汽车于2004在台湾推出的一辆基于第九代美规Galant重新设计的车型，可视为美规Galant的双胞胎车款。
拍摄于2022年8月！

Mitsubishi Galant
Since 1969 Mitsubishi has developed nine generations Glant, the first eight which are positioned as high-performance sports sedan. In these eight generations, the mainland imported five and eight generation, Taiwan Zhonghua automobile produced eight generation, Hong Kong and Macao imported many generation. At The 2003 New York Auto Show, Mitsubishi North America announced the ninth generation Galant. In November 2006, Southeast Auto introduced Galant . However, the version is not the original version which produced in the North American market, this is the Grunder version of Taiwan Mitsubishi, which is a redesigned model based on the ninth generation of American Galant launched by Japan Mitsubishi Motors and Taiwan china-motor in 2004. It can be regarded as the twin of American Standard Galant.
