路虎 揽胜三代
要说揽胜就要先说路虎，而要说路虎就要先说罗孚和利兰！
1877年，罗孚(Rover)公司成立于英国。二战后，全球迎来了一波黄金发展时期，民间对汽车的需求也快速增长。罗孚公司于是基于威利斯Jeep的底盘和传统系统，配合铝合金车身并大量采用罗孚P-3的零件，在1948年举行的阿姆斯特丹车展上正式发布-Land Rover Series I ，这也标志着路虎品牌（Land Rover）正式诞生。
1968年，彼时的世界第四大汽车厂英国汽车公司(British Motor Corporation Limited)（旗下拥有莫里斯(Morris)、莱利(Riley)、MG、沃尔斯利(Wolseley) 、奥斯汀(Austin)、阿尔比恩（Albion）、标准-凯旋（Standard-Triumph）、AEC、Scammell）与罗孚合并为英国利兰汽车公司(British Leyland Motor Corporation)。1970年，罗孚的越野车部门-路虎，推出揽胜”(Range Rover)车型。
1975 年，经营不善的利兰汽车被强制国有化。1978年，利兰被重组为 4 个部分：英国利兰轿车（BL Cars）、英国利兰卡车与巴士（BL Trucks and Bus）、特殊产品（Special Products）与英国利兰国际（BL International）。同年，作为越野车部门的路虎从罗孚独立出来成为一个单独的品牌，而揽胜则成为了路虎旗下的一个产品序列。

The company Rover was founded in England in 1877. After the WWII, the world ushered in a wave of golden development, cars demand from private also increased rapidly. Rover started delelop a car based on Jeep Willis chassis and traditional system, with aluminum body and a large number of Rover P-3 parts, Rover company officially launched the Land Rover Series I at the 1948 Amsterdam Motor Show, which also marked the official birth of Land Rover brand.

In 1968, the world's fourth largest car factory-British Motor Corporation Limited (incloud Morris, Riley, MG, Wolseley,Austin, Albion, Standard-Triumph, AEC, Scammell and Rover and so on) merged Rover became British Leyland Motor Corporation. In 1970, Rover's off-road division department-Land Rover, introduced the Range Rover model.
In 1975, the ailing Leyland Motor was to be nationalized. In 1978, Leyland was reorganised into four parts: British Leyland Cars, British Leyland Trucks and Bus, Special Products and British Leyland International. In the same year, Land Rover became a separate brand from Rover as an off-road vehicle division apartment, and Range Rover became a product line within Land Rover.
