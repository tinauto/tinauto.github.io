【老旧车】标致 307sw\Peugeot 307sw

2022-06-18 PEUGEOT ， 老旧车

标致307sw
2001年别克引进欧宝巴西公司的三厢可赛并改名赛欧在国内销售，凭借低廉的价格（10万）、合资品牌的身份和较高的配置（双气囊+abs）一炮而红，旅行版的销量在最多时曾经占到过同期销量的40%。如此之高的销量给了广大汽车厂一种“中国市场对旅行车有巨大需求”假象，在此之后众多品牌开始向这中国市场导入旅行车（见下表 ）。标致就是其中一员，标致在2007年开始向中国市场倒入307sw，两款车售价分别为23.98万和26.5万。
但是事与愿违，这些想要在中国市场推广旅行车的努力无一例外以失败告终。随着suv的蓬勃发展，中国市场上的中低价位旅行车就更是变得凤毛麟角了。2022年，斯柯达明锐旅行、大众c-trek、宝骏rc5w相继停产之后，20万以下的平价旅行车就只剩下福克斯旅行版和宝骏310w两款在勉强支撑。
拍摄于2022年5月！

btw：别克赛欧并不是欧宝可赛第一次进入中国，江门美鹿汽车厂在1993年（欧宝可赛二代发布的当年）以CKD散件组装的形式生产过二代欧宝可赛，命名为美鹿jj7090

Peugeot 307sw
In 2001, Buick introduced Opel-Brazil corsa and renamed it Sail and sold it in China. It is became hot with low price (100,000 yuan) joint venture brand identity and high configuration (double air bags + ABS), the wagon version once accounted for about 40% of the sales volume in the same period. Such high sales data gave automakers the illusion that "there was a huge demand for station wagons in The Chinese market", after which many brands began to introduce wagons to the Chinese market (see table below). Peugeot is one of them, and began to sell the 307SW in 2007, priced at 239,800 yuan and 265,000 yuan .
However, these efforts to promote wagon in the chinese market have all failed. With the booming development of suv, middle andlow price wagon in the chinese market have become very rare. In 2022, Skoda Octavia wagon, VW C-Trek, Baojun rc5w have stopped production one by one, the wagon following 200,000 yuan only left Ford focus wagon and Baojun 310W.
Shooting in May 2022！

btw: Buick Excellent is not the first time that Opel corsa enter China. In 1993 (Opel corsa II was released in this year), Jiangmen 美鹿 （literally-beauitful deer） company produced the Opel corsa ii by the ckd and named it JJ7090
