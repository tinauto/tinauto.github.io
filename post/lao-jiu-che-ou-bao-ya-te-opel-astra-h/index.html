欧宝 雅特三代
欧宝雅特迪三代车型在2004年面世，在欧宝的内部代号为A04，是基于欧洲通用前置前驱T平台开发而来。在欧洲这款车一共有六种款式，2005年开始国内陆续引进了其中四个款式（两厢三门、两厢五门、三厢四门、三厢硬顶敞篷），旅行车、封闭货车则没有引进。

Opel Astra H
The Opel Astra H was introduced in 2004, named A04 in Opel internally, and it is based on the GE-European front drive T platform. In Europe, there are altogether six styles of this car, four styles of which have been introduced in China since 2005 (three-door hatchback、 five-door hatchback、four-door seden、hardtop convertible), wagon and van have not been introduced.
