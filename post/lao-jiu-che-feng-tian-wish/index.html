【老旧车】丰田 Wish\Toyota Wish

2022-04-09 TOYOTA ， 老旧车

丰田 Wish
在10年左右国内有过一阵引进mpv的小热潮，马自达引进了马自达5、丰田引进了ez\wish、雪铁龙引进了c4大毕加索、欧宝引进赛飞利\麦瑞纳、起亚引进佳乐，今天要说的就是丰田wish。
作为车圈的老大哥，看到其他厂家纷纷引进mpv到中国销售丰田也绝不能闲着，于是丰田中国从丰田英国引进了ez并且进行了国产，售价为15万-24万；从丰田日本引进了wish，以纯进口方式销售，售价27.8万。但是很遗憾，这款售价27.8万的mpv销量和同一价位的汉兰达相比毫无优势，最终铩羽而归。
值得一提的是，大陆地区销售的wish并不是日本制造的，而是台湾制造的。但是，因为中国大陆地区实施“进口许可制度，而丰田台湾的“国瑞丰田工厂”并不在许可目录内。再加上台湾和大陆在车辆转向灯等设备上有不同法律规范，于是丰田中国采用了由第三方改装之后再进口的进口方式，因此在wish的铭牌上标注的是其改装厂“福祐汽车”而不是生产厂“国瑞丰田”
拍摄于2022年3月！

btw:2010年，福特中国曾经进口过少量的台湾福特六和生产的翼虎，其也是由福祐进行的改装

Toyota Wish
In 2010 , China has a small wave of introduction of MPV，Mazda introduced Mazda 5, Toyota introduced EZ and Wish, Citroen introduced C4 Picasso, Opel introduced zafira and meriva, Kia introduced carens, and today i will talking about Toyota Wish.
As a big brother , Toyota could not stay idle when other manufacturers introduced MPV and sell it in China . Therefore, Toyota-China introduced EZ from Toyota UK and made it in GAC-Toyota with a price of 150,000-240,000 yuan. Wish was imported from Toyota-Japan and sold with a price of 278,000 yuan. Unfortunately, the 278,000 yuan MPV failed to sell as well as the Highlander at the same price.
It is worth to say ，the Wish sold in China is not made in Japan, it is made in Taiwan. However, because of the “China Import licensing system” ，Toyota Taiwan's "Kuozui Factory" is not in the list. Addation, Taiwan and China have different legal on vehicles equipment such as turn light and others , Toyota China import it after Third-party car modification factory modified that car, so ，on the nameplate we can see the name of made factory is "Fuyou(福祐)" rather than the reality manufacturer "Toyota-Taiwan Kuozui "
Shooting in March 2022!

btw：In 2010, ford china imported a little of Escape maded by Taiwan Ford LioHo, which was also modified by Fuyou
