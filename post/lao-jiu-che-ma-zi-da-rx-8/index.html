【老旧车】马自达 RX-8\Mazda RX-8

2024-02-10 MAZDA ， 老旧车

马自达 RX-8
RX-8是马自达最后一款搭载转子发动机的车型（不考虑 Demio EV），也是全球所有汽车品牌中最后一款使用转子发动机量产车型。

2005年上海车展期间，马自达公司宣布，将向中国市场投放的4种新车型，分别是：RX-8、Mazda6掀背式轿车、Mazda6旅行车以及Tribute（与福特翼虎escape、水星水手mariner同平台）。同年，马自达正式向中国出口6mt版本的RX-8，售价为38.5万元。

2008年改款之后（最大特征就是变成了4圆尾灯）国内则引进了6mt-售价38万和 6at-售价39万。
拍摄于2024年1月！

===
Mazda RX-8
RX-8 is the last Mazda model to used rotary engine (not includ Demio EV), and the last production model of any car brand in the world to use a rotary engine.

During the 2005 Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhitition, Mazda announced that they will bring four models into China-market: the RX-8、the Mazda6 fastback、the Mazda6 wagon, and the Tribute (use the same platform as the Ford escape and Mercury mariner). In the same year, Mazda officially exported RX-8 6mt to China at a price of 385,000 Yuan.

After the 2008 revision (the biggest change is use 4 round rear lights), Mazda introduction 6mt(380,000 Yuan) and 6at(390,000 Yuan) into China.
Shooting in January 2024!
